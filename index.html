// About the Application

What is Mr.Baig Downloader?

Powerful. Portable. Professional.

Mr.Baig Downloader is a fully-featured Windows desktop application engineered in Python, built around the legendary yt-dlp download engine — one of the most actively maintained video download libraries in the world.

Unlike traditional downloaders that require cumbersome installation processes, Mr.Baig Downloader is a fully portable application — simply run the executable and start downloading. No registry entries, no system changes, just pure downloading power.

Under the hood, it leverages FFmpeg — the industry-standard multimedia framework — to seamlessly merge separate video and audio streams into perfectly synchronized, high-quality output files. This means you always get the best possible resolution your device supports.

Whether you need a video in MP4, WebM, MKV, or just the audio in MP3, AAC, FLAC, Mr.Baig Downloader handles it effortlessly. Choose your resolution, pick your format, and download.

Quick Support FAQ

Common questions and answers to help you get started quickly.

Do I need to install anything?
No. Mr.Baig Downloader is fully portable. Just download the .exe and run it directly — no installation wizard, no admin rights required.
Which websites are supported?
Powered by yt-dlp, it supports 1000+ sites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, Vimeo, Dailymotion, and many more.
Can I download in 4K?
Yes! If the source video is available in 4K, you can download it. FFmpeg handles merging the video and audio streams automatically.

Completely safe and ad-free. No bundled software, no telemetry, no background processes. Pure downloader utility.
